Privacy Policy for Thinkify Labs

Last updated: May 16, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up to explore opportunities on our platform, we collect:

  • Contact information: Your full name, email address, and phone number.
  • Professional details: Your résumé/CV, work history, skills, portfolio links, and any other information you choose to share in your profile.
  • Usage data: How you interact with our website and services, collected via cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Match you with opportunities: Our team leverages your profile to recommend roles that fit your background and goals.
  • Communicate with you: We send updates on application status, interview schedules, and new job matches via email, SMS, and calls.
  • Improve our service: We analyze usage patterns to refine features and ensure a seamless experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations: We retain data as needed for regulatory requirements.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tools to remember your preferences, track site performance, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings through your browser; disabling cookies may limit certain features.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We will never sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Partner startups for recruitment purposes.
  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Data Retention

We keep your profile information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days unless required to retain it for legal reasons.

6. Security

  • Industry-standard encryption (TLS) for data in transit.
  • Restricted access to authorized team members only.
  • Regular audits of systems and processes.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • Access & update: View and edit your profile anytime in account settings.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails or SMS by clicking "Unsubscribe" or replying "STOP".
  • Data deletion: Email us at privacy@thinkifylabs.com to request data erasure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are for individuals aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have, please contact us to remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy for new features or legal requirements. We’ll post revisions here and notify you via email if changes are significant.
